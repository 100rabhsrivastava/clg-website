About us



About Sevadal Community


Sevadal Education Society was founded in the year 1958, by great patriot Late Shri Sulemankhan Pathan Guruji alongwith his colleagues of Sevadal cadre with very limited resources and extreme hardships. He was a dedicated Freedom fighter who firmly believed in Gandhian philosophy. Education not only improves one’s life and helps in character building but also plays a major role in Nation building. With this vision, to train young minds with patriotism and national values, to educate and to develop self confidence in them, this Society was established. With his strong will, commitment, strength, determination and the strategic support of Hon’able Shri Keshaoraoji Shende, who also happens to be the ex-president of sevadal education society, the society has developed from its humble beginning into a mammoth entity. The Society conceptualized its journey by starting Vidarbha Buniyadi Primary School, Middle School, High School and Vidarbha Buniyadi Junior College to cater the demand and need of education in the backward areas of East Nagpur.

Vission & Mission

Our Mission is to Nurture the students with
• The Modern Scientific Approach, Digitalization and E-learning.
• The Vision for Healthy and Clean Nation.
• The Education to become Eco-friendly, Socio friendly and Secular.
• The Historical, Cultural, Intellectual and Spiritual Heritage of India.
• The Vision to Perceive Society as our Best friend, Philosopher, Guide and Resource.
• Nationalism with the International Outlook for Global Harmony and Integration.
• The Strength to become Confident, Competent, Hardworking and Creative.

1) To extend education to all levels of society.
2) To make students achieve Morality, Ethics and Values.
3) To attain students’ Intellectual, Physical, Mental, Cultural and Social Development.
4) To develop Modern and Scientific Perceptive among students.
5) To strengthen students’ skillsets through Digitalization and E-learning.
6) To make students aware about Individual Cleanliness, Health and Sustainable Environment.
7) To implement Clean Environment, Clean School and Clean India mission.
8) To cultivate Indian Culture, Secularism, National Integrity and National spirit to imbibe constitutional values.



Directors Message
