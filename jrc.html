Mr. Dilip Tadas
Headmaster of Vidarbha Buniyadi Junior College
In all my 27 years of teaching experience, spending my time in the classrooms with my students and then working intensely with my colleagues in order to provide A-class education to our students have always been pleasurable moments for me. I have also worked as head Critic in Nagpur Division Board for Marathi subject in 2020 and as Paper setter in 2019 which contributed in me becoming a confident and more experienced person for my team.

Freatures

Arts (Marathi medium)
Commerce (Marathi medium)
MCVC (H.S.C Vocational) (Marathi medium) with Trades Accounting and Office management, Horticulture and Crop Science
Science (General subjects- Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry and Physics with Bifocal subjects like Fisheries and Electronics)

M.C.V.C course offers students commercial approach to earning while learning and encourages students to start their smallscale business.
It’s aimed towards entrepreneurship and startups.
Science course helps to appear for medical, engineering and scientific research entrance examination.
Commerce and Arts helps students to get into fields like Law, Banking, Statistical jobs, Literature, etc.

Notes:
Any one bifocal subject can be opted.
It will be against 2 subjects
Second language (Marathi / Sanskrit / IT) will be dropped.
For Electronics : Biology and Second language (Marathi / Sanskrit / IT) will be dropped.
For Fisheries (FWFC) : Maths and Second language (Marathi / Sanskrit / IT) will be dropped.
