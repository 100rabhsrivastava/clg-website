Academics



Academics

Primary School

Learning is Fun” is our motto and we make our children believe in the same. Playing with numbers and dancing along with alphabets , making friendship with languages and exploring the science are some of the things our students get along in time.

Middle School

Nurturing and forming the base of innocent students promoted from primary school and making them ready for senior years is what we do! We believe that “Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.”

High School

Teaching and guiding to the right way to our teenager students is what we practice in our high school. We make them believe how important their life is and how education will take them to heights. The strong foundation we give in basic science and mathematics helps our students in years ahead.

Junior College

Our junior college which consists of XI and XII classes aims not only for excellent education of board level but also guide and motivate our students for various competitive exams in the field of science, humanities, commerce, etc.



Facility


Library

To create interest among students, to maintain their hobbies and develop their all-round personality we have a collection of about 10,000 books on literature, science, commerce, technology, philosophy, biographies, history, politics, etc. Our library also includes newspapers, magazines, research papers and novels. Reading room is also allotted in our huge library premises.
Know More

Labs

We at Vidarbha Buniyadi provide a range of eight practical labs to our students keeping in mind the quality of equipments and safety measures taken during special practicals. Spacious, highly updated and well furnished lab environment is provided with independent floor allotted to each lab.
Know More

Transport

Our buses also ply for students in need of transport at a very reasonable cost. Safety, hygiene and comfort are services utmost taken care of. Students coming from remote areas like bahadura, panchgaon, vihirgaon, kalamna, dighori, narsala, etc are really happy with this service along with their parents satisfactory reviews.

Mid-day Meal

We strictly follow the rules and regulation laid by Government of India in order to provide regular, hygienic, freshly-cooked and a good amount of food to all of our students from class 1 to 8 in recess period which works in two shifts. Nearly 1000 students are benefitted on a daily basis.
