Mrs. Sandhya S. Gaidhane
Headmistress of Vidarbha Buniyadi Primary School
I have completed 26 years in teaching field. I am working on a post of H.M. since last 3 years. Our school has Marathi and lower English medium in our primary department i.e. nursery, KG 1, KG2 and standard 1st to 4th. In every session we arrange various types of academic programs to develop students’ overall progress. Our teachers often organize pep-talk for students and focus on each individual and play an essential role in shaping the future of the students. We improve educational curriculum in mental, physical and social aspect and always encourage the students. Every student achieves better education, good qualities and memorable experience from our school.
